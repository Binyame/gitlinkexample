About Megenagna Ethiopian Market

We are located at the heart of Silver Spring. We carry authentic Ethiopian foods, spices, and traditional clothes. We are well-know for our freshly baked enjera and bread. The enjera you can find in our store is made of 100 % pure teff which is know for its health benefit and glutten free. We also carry fresh meat (kurt).

Dimbelal(Coriander)

Coriander is a spice produced from the round, tan-colored seeds of the coriander plant (Coriandrum sativum), which is a member of the parsley family.

Cumin,Ginger,Tumeric,Curry

Rosemary

Rosemary is a fragrant evergreen herb native to the Mediterranean. It is used as a culinary condiment, to make bodily perfumes, and for its potential health benefits.
